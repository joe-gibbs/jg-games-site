FALL OF AN EMPIRE

A Survival Strategy Epic

Your empire is slowly falling. Bickering vassals and barbarian hordes threaten to bring about its destruction. Thousands of years of history have led you to this point, can you be the one to halt its decline?

Rule a Crumbling Empire
Command Epic Battles
Explore a Massive World
Complex Character Interactions

Unlike traditional strategy games, you can't win. Your empire will fall—it's just a matter of how long you can delay the inevitable.

VISIT OFFICIAL SITE

Defend Your Empire

Manage the Empire's dwindling resources. Upgrade settlements to withstand those who seek to destroy you. Command your Imperial legions, recruit armies, and crush your foes—though be sure your strategies aren't outmatched by your enemies.

A Massive World

Rule almost 150 settlements with millions of citizens. Feel the gravity of your rule as you watch seasons change through harsh winters and bountiful springs. Experience a truly immersive imperial world.

Unique Battles

Fight enemies on a real-time strategic level with a unique timed turn-based battle system. Choose which conflicts deserve your attention while waging war across all points of your empire simultaneously.

Expansive Lore

Uncover the history of the world through events and dialog. Learn the secrets of your forebears and the complex world you inhabit. Do you have what it takes to make the decisions of an Emperor?
